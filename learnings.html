Final Reflection

This internship at Cognifyz Technologies has been an incredibly rewarding experience that accelerated my growth as a frontend developer. The hands-on projects pushed me out of my comfort zone and helped me develop both technical skills and professional maturity.

Most importantly, this experience confirmed my passion for frontend development and gave me the confidence to take on more complex projects. I'm excited to continue building on these foundations as I progress in my career.
